Mama Maggy

Mama Maggy is an international pizza chain, operating in 75 countries with 10,000 outlets. Mama Maggy and its franchise partners employ 200,000 staff and had revenues of $2.0 billion last year. Mama Maggy was founded in 1970 and its headquaters is in Pasadena, CA. According to wikipedia the company name derives from queen Margherita - in 1889.
